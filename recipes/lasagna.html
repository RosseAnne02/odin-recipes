Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (about 450g) ground beef or Italian sausage (or a combination)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps for preparation

  1. Prepare Meat Sauce: Brown ground beef, sauté onions and garlic, then add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, red wine,
    oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Make Ricotta Filling: Combine ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Cook Lasagna Noodles: Follow package instructions, then drain.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil, bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes, uncover, and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until cheese is
    golden.
  5. Rest and Serve: Let it rest for 10-15 minutes, then garnish with fresh basil or parsley before serving. Enjoy your lasagna!
