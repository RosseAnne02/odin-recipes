Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • For the Cheesecake Filling:
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Crust:
    • crushed graham crackers, sugar, and melted butter.
    • Press the mixture into the base of a greased springform pan.
    • Beat cream cheese until smooth.
    • Add sugar, vanilla extract, and eggs, mixing until well combined
    • Pour the filling onto the crust
  2. Bake:
    • Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
    • Bake the cheesecake for about 50-60 minutes until the center is set.
  3. Cool and Refrigerate:
    • Let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door ajar, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  4. Optional Toppings:
    1. Top with fruit compote, chocolate ganache, or whipped cream before serving.
